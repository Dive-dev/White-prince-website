Vedmenko production

Vedmenko Production - is a young, promising Video production. Our team makes creative and high-quality photo and video content that popularizes your brand, attracts a large number of customers and gives a lot of positive emotions from the finished product.

I knew each of this team personally. And I could not even think that these guys would combine their knowledge and implement such a global project. First, they asked me to create a website for them, and then they asked me to join their membership.

The creation of the site started, not so quickly, because the designer we asked to draw this site was very creative and did his job diligently.

I waited for the first template for almost a week. And so I broke into work and at that moment I thought: "With such a speed we will not finish even in 6 months!" and invited his friend to do this work together (He was remotely familiar with the layout, but decided to try).

But my partner was impatient and soon left the work area, but I was not upset with him. And I had to sit down to finish the job myself. The first thing I had to do was open the part of the code that my colleague wrote. When I went there, I first realized how hard it is to understand someone else's code.

After all, before that I had never come across the work of other people. And I had to rewrite everything. It took not a small amount of time and now the second week has come. 2 pages were completed. There was no time limit on this order, but I really wanted to finish it as quickly as possible due to the amount of problems I encountered.

I took matters into my own hands and began to write, did all the layout and sent it for confirmation.

The customer asked for some changes. There were not many edits, but I had to sweat. And now the layout is ready, the customer approved.

I started writing the backend. My task was to write a function to send the form to the mail.

I opened the Django documentation and began to read, I did not understand from the first reading.

I opened the articles, read them, did everything as described in them, and something is already being written to the console, I thought that I was on the right track, went further, registered an account in mailgun, entered all the data and it worked, but not for long.

I thought something was wrong and came to the conclusion that I posted my secret keys on the git and they blocked me. And so it was.

I had to change my account and that's it.

Finally completed the job within a month. Although it could be done in a week.
